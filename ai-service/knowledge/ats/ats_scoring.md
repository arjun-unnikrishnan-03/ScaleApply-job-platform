# ATS Scoring Guidelines

The Applicant Tracking System (ATS) evaluation is a critical first step. It is an objective measure of the raw overlap between a candidate's profile and the job description.

## Scoring Weights
1. **Hard Skills (40%)**: Exact or highly synonymous matches for required technologies (e.g., React, Python, AWS).
2. **Experience Level (30%)**: Does the candidate's total years of relevant experience meet the minimum threshold?
3. **Education/Certifications (15%)**: Do they possess the required degree or mandatory industry certifications?
4. **Keywords/Context (15%)**: Contextual matches for industry terms, methodologies (Agile, Scrum), and domain expertise.

## Disqualification Criteria
- **Visa Requirements**: If the job strictly requires US Citizenship and the candidate requires sponsorship.
- **Location**: If the job is strict on-site in a specific city and the candidate is remote-only without willingness to relocate.
